Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I get a verse for my current mood?

Open the app, select your mood, and Mood Verse will generate a Bible verse to match your feelings.

2. Can I choose my preferred Bible version?

Yes! Go to Settings → Bible Version, and select from multiple versions available.

3. How can I share verses with friends?

Tap the share icon on any verse to send it via messaging apps, email, or social media.
